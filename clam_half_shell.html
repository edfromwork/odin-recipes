Clams on the Half Shell

A childhood classic from my days living on the Connecticut shoreline.

5 min Prep time

15 min Total time

4 servings

Ingredients

  • fresh live clams
  • lemon
  • tobasco
  • cocktail sauce

Steps

  1. Step 1

    Using a clam knife, shuck your clams.

  2. Step 2

    Place on serving platter covered in crushed ice.

  3. Step 3

    Serve with lemon wedges and tobasco and cocktail sauce.
